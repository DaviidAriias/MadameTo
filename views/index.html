Grammar

To form the present continuous, the auxiliary verb "to be" and the verb + ing are used.

The grammar structure is:
In affirmative: Subject+ verb to be+ verb+ ing
example: he is learning
In negative: Subject+ verb to be+ not+ verb+ ing
example: I am not doing my homework
In interrogative: verb to be+ subject + verb+ ing + ?
example: Are they playing the piano?

Auxiliars:
I: am
She/he/it: is
They/we/you: are
